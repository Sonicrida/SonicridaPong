Sonicrida Pong

Welcome to Sonicrida Pong! This is a game I made to get familiar with Phaser, an awesome javascript game development framework. It was made with the help of a lovely tutorial written by Edvin Antonov. It's not perfect but it's a working game of pong written completely with javascript. The music is from Mario and Luigi: Dream Team.

How to play:

Click to start the game and move your mouse to hit the ball back at the opponent and try to get it past them.
